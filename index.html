Easy Blackberry Icebox Cake

This dreamy and creamy dessert is perfect for those hot summer days where you want to make a nice dessert for the family without all the hassle.

Cheryl Norris © 2022 CCO

Ingredients

This is for 8 servings

  • ✔ 2 pints blackberries, divided
  • ✔ ½ cup blackberry jam
  • ✔ 1 lemon
  • ✔ 2 cups chilled heavy cream
  • ✔ ¼ cup powdered sugar
  • ✔ 8 oz. vanilla wafers, divided
  • ✔ Pinch of kosher salt

Preparation! Step by Step

Step 1

Measure out 2 cups blackberries from 2 pints...

Step 2

Add ½ cup blackberry jam...

Step 3

Using an electric mixer, beat 2 cups heavy cream...

Step 4

Beat in ¼ cup powdered sugar...

Step 5

Line a medium bowl with plastic wrap...

Step 6

Spoon about ¼ cup berry mixture over wafers...

Step 7

Spread ½ cup of the remaining whipped cream evenly...

Step 8

Bring up plastic overhang to cover and chill at least 24 hours.

Step 9

Unwrap plastic from the top of the cake...

Step 10

Using a whisk, beat the reserved whipped cream...

Step 11

Cut reserved berries in half lengthwise and arrange them on top.

Slice cake into 8 pieces to serve and Enjoy!!
